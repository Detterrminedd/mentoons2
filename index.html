We at mentoons are here to mentor through cartoons! Focusing on concepts such as social media de-adiction, we use psychology-based modules and cartoons to influence and change maladaptive behaviour in your child. Mentoons has got your back, whether you're a parent looking for a holistic yet fun growth environment for your 6 year old or even if you're a 20-year old looking for career guidance!

Based on the “9 Human Intelligences” Theory , Our workshops are designed and delivered by Psychologists. Our fun sessions will help children learn about thoughts, interactions, reactions, making friends, self awareness and more!

Our aim is to have a positive impact on children by first positively impacting their parents


  • 🌎 Our workshops create a fun learning environment for parents.

  • 🌎 understand how to manage areas like pampering kids.

Through our workshops you discover an environment which is best for your child where they can explore their curiosity, grow and enjoy while having fun!
